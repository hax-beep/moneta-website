About Podar

For over eight decades, S.P. Mandali's R.A. Podar College of Commerce and Economics (Empowered Autonomous) has stood as a pillar of academic excellence and transformative education in India. Established in 1941, Podar College began with 150 students and has since evolved into one of the nation's most respected institutions recognized with an 'A+' grade by NAAC for its commitment to quality, innovation, and impact. But Podar is more than an institution; it's a legacy. A space where tradition meets innovation, where discipline meets curiosity, and where commerce becomes more than a subject, it becomes a lens through which students understand and shape the world. Guided by the ethos of "Nurturing Intellect and Creating Personalities," the college is deeply invested in the holistic development of its students. Here, academic rigor is enriched by leadership opportunities, cultural experiences, and real-world exposure cultivating not just graduates, but visionaries, changemakers, and ethical leaders. Rooted in values of integrity, knowledge, and excellence, Podar doesn't just prepare students for exams it prepares them for life. A Podarite carries forward a legacy of purpose and progress, empowered to challenge norms, lead with conscience, and rise to every occasion. Because at Podar, greatness isn't a destination, it's a tradition.
